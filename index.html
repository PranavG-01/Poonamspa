About




About Poonam Wason: As an esthetician with over 17 years of experience, Poonam brings her passion for skincare to every treatment. With roots tracing back to India and a diploma from Empire Beauty School in Louisville, Kentucky, she's mastered the art of enhancing natural beauty. Poonam not only excels in her craft but also manages her role as a devoted single mother of 2. With almost 2 years of running her own business, Poonam's expertise shines through in her diverse range of treatments: from personalized facials addressing specific concerns to blissful relaxation therapies. Poonam's genuine commitment to client satisfaction ensures a client's experience is not only transformative but deeply rejuvenating.
